# DSAR Operational Checklist

## Intake

- Record receipt date/time
- Assign case ID
- Record requester identity and contact details
- Record jurisdiction and likely deadline
- Assess whether identity verification is needed

## Scope

- Capture exact wording of the request
- Identify clarifications needed
- Record date clarification was requested and received
- Define systems, business units, and custodians in scope

## Search

- List each system searched
- Record search terms / filters used
- Record who performed the search
- Record dates and outcomes
- Keep originals intact

## Review

- Identify third-party data
- Identify privileged or exempt material
- Mark redactions and reasons
- Separate raw and disclosure-ready material

## Response

- Summarize searches performed
- List disclosed materials
- Explain limits, exemptions, and redactions where appropriate
- Record send date and delivery channel
