## Material Rules

### Surfaces
- Translucent glass with backdrop blur (8-12px)
- Hairline borders (1px, translucent)
- Layered, not flat
- Subtle gradients (≤8% opacity)

### Buttons
- Low-height, refined (40px standard)
- Soft radius (12px)
- Glow on focus, not giant shadows
- Primary feels lit from within

### Inputs
- Soft dark wells
- Thin illuminated border on focus
- No thick outlines
- Placeholder calm and muted

## Motion Patterns

### Panel Slide
- 20px offset start
- 300-400ms smooth ease
- Opacity + transform

### Card Reveal
- 12px vertical, scale 0.98→1
- 250-350ms
- Staggered (50ms per item)

### Modal
- 24px vertical, scale 0.96→1
- Backdrop first (300ms)
- Surface follows (350-450ms)

### Button Press
- Scale 0.97 on active
- 100ms fast
- Minimal bounce

## Do's & Don'ts

### Visual Do's
✅ Dark mode first
✅ Generous spacing
✅ Subtle accents
✅ Layered surfaces
✅ Smooth motion
✅ High contrast (softened)

### Visual Don'ts
❌ Light mode as flagship
❌ Cramped layouts
❌ Bright neon takeovers
❌ Flat surfaces
❌ Bouncy animations
❌ Harsh #000 vs #FFF

### Brand Do's
✅ Unified product family
✅ Consistent tokens
✅ Shared motion language
✅ Product-specific accents
✅ Editorial spacing

### Brand Don'ts
❌ Random startup aesthetics
❌ Inconsistent radii
❌ Dashboard clutter
❌ Cyberpunk vibes
❌ Gamer styling
❌ Industrial clichés
