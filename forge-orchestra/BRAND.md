# ForgeOrchestra Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Positioning

ForgeOrchestra sits at the intersection of:

- **Apple** — Immaculate spacing, material confidence, reduction
- **OpenAI** — Intelligent surfaces, calm modernity, subtle depth
- **Linear** — Precision and discipline
- **Notion at its best** — Clarity and hierarchy
- **Arc / Modern OS** — Fluid motion and ambient intelligence

**Emotional Result:** "Serious power, without visual stress."

---

## 2. Core Concept

**Orchestrated flow through forged structure.**

The visuals combine two tensions:
- Precision geometry
- Soft intelligent motion

**Think:** Machined aluminum meets ambient neural interface.

---

## 3. Logo Direction

### Principles

**Do NOT:**
- Busy symbols with pipes, gears, nodes, music notes, AI swirls
- Cyberpunk aesthetics
- Gamer-ish styling
- Overly industrial clichés

**Do:**
- Minimal and architectural
- Monoline geometric emblem
- Folded frame mark
- Orbital grid mark
- Refined wordmark with compact emblem

### Wordmark

**Typography qualities:**
- Neutral but premium
- Geometric without feeling cold
- Highly legible
- Contemporary
- Slightly condensed or tightly controlled spacing

**Format:** `ForgeOrchestra` (not FORGEORCHESTRA, not split unless editorial)

**Product convention:**
```
ForgeOrchestra
ForgeCalendar
ForgePipeline
ForgeDisplay
ForgeOpenClaw
```

## 5. Material Design Language

**Visual Material Stack:**
- Matte dark foundation
- Translucent glass panels
- Subtle brushed-metal highlights
- Diffused glow edges
- Ultra-soft shadows
- Very restrained gradients

**Surface Rules:**
- Cards feel layered, not flat
- Edges softly radiused (18–28px)
- Borders hairline and translucent
- Internal glow subtle and cool-toned
- Gradients barely perceptible in static states

---

## 6. Layout Philosophy

**ForgeOrchestra feels:**
- Spacious
- Editorial
- System-aware

**NOT:**
- Noisy BI dashboard from 2017
- Everything-at-once
- Dashboard-heavy by default

**Principles:**
- Strong margins
- Lots of negative space
- Clear module grouping
- Asymmetrical but balanced compositions
- One dominant focal point per screen
- Progressive disclosure

**Design Rhythm:**
Breathing room → Dense utility → Breathing room

---

## 7. Shape Language

**Characteristics:**
- Rounded rectangles with disciplined radii
- Arcs, rings, and pathways
- Thin connector lines
- Layered grids
- Concentric framing
- Precise alignment

**Avoid:**
- Jagged polygons
- Aggressive diagonals
- Overly mechanical cog references
- Cartoon-ish abstraction

---

## 8. Typography System

**Primary Sans:** Neutral grotesk / neo-grotesk / modern UI sans

**Hierarchy:**
- Hero titles: Elegant, large, minimal, 600 weight
- Section headers: Crisp and slightly compact
- Labels: Subtle uppercase or small-title style
- Data states: Mono, restrained
- Product labels: Consistent title case

**Tone:**
- Calm
- Exact
- Premium
- Controlled

Nothing shouty. Nothing over-stylised.

## 10. Motion Language

**Feels:**
- Fluid
- Responsive
- Intelligent
- Restrained
- Slightly cinematic

**NOT:**
- Bouncy
- Gimmicky
- Full of overshoot and dopamine spam

**Behaviors:**
- Panels glide and settle softly
- Highlights track cursor subtly
- Gradients breathe very gently
- Routes animate in controlled pulses
- State changes feel like system transitions

**Principle:** *Everything should feel like it already knows where it is going.*

**Layered Atmospheric Depth:**
- Dark matte base

## 13. UI Surface Look

**UI Characteristics:**
- Floating workspace shells
- Intelligent side rails
- Layered panels
- Command-aware status bars
- Crisp segmented controls
- Slightly translucent overlays
- Elegant system cards
- Premium search/command palette surfaces

**Buttons:**
- Low-height, refined
- Soft radius
- Glow on focus, not giant shadows

**Inputs:**
- Soft dark wells

**Tables/Lists:**
- Airy rows
- Ultra-thin separators
- Subtle zebra or hover elevation
- Data dense, never cramped

## 15. Design Tokens (Hard Rules)

### Radii
- Small elements: `12px`
- Cards/containers: `20–24px`
- Modal/shell surfaces: `28px`

### Borders
- 1px translucent borders
- Occasional inner highlight line for active surfaces

### Shadows
- Very soft, diffused
- Low opacity
- Layered, not cartoon drop shadows

### Blur
- Restrained
- Only where it improves depth
- Avoid blur soup

### Spacing
- 8pt grid base
- Premium spacing scale
- Never cram cards together

### Contrast
- High enough for clarity
- Softened by material layering
- Avoid harsh pure white against pure black
