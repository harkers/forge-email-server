# ForgeOrchestra

**The Operating Layer for Modern Workflows**

---

## Brand Essence

ForgeOrchestra is the master environment — the operating layer where workflows, systems, automations, dashboards, and specialist products converge.

If the child products are instruments, ForgeOrchestra is the conductor, score, and stage.

### Core Attributes

- **Intelligent** — System-aware, predictive, calm
- **Controlled** — Precise, disciplined, orchestrated
- **Elegant** — Refined, premium, architectural
- **Quietly Premium** — Dense with capability, light in appearance

---

## Visual Direction: Obsidian Intelligence

**Design Formula:**
- Dark architectural base
- Luminous intelligent accents
- Soft glass materials
- Disciplined typography
- Ambient motion
- Unified product family rules

**Feels Like:** Apple × OpenAI × Enterprise Command Center

## Product Family

ForgeOrchestra governs a suite of precision tools:

| Product | Accent | Motif |
|---------|--------|-------|
| **ForgeCalendar** | Ice Cyan | Rings, time arcs, cadence |
| **ForgePipeline** | Emerald Teal | Branching routes, staged nodes |
| **ForgeDisplay** | Broadcast Amber | Framed canvases, panels |
| **ForgeOpenClaw** | Neural Violet | Orchestration mesh, command lines |

All products share the same architecture, differentiated by accent and motif.
