# DevForge: Project Foundry
## ForgeRisk Specification

Status: Planning  
Date: 2026-03-28

## What ForgeRisk Is

ForgeRisk is the dedicated risk cycle, register, and mitigation history domain.

It keeps risk visible throughout the lifecycle instead of treating risk as a static checklist completed once and forgotten.

## Purpose

- identify risks early
- evaluate likelihood and impact
- log mitigations
- preserve mitigation history
- support re-evaluation when delivery changes

## Core Artefacts

- risk register
- risk evaluation record
- mitigation log
- risk ownership mapping

## Triggers

ForgeRisk should be revisited when:
- major design changes occur
- governance gates identify concerns
- review fails
- fix loops begin
- release readiness changes the exposure profile

## Risk Types

Expected categories include:
- privacy
- security
- delivery
- operational
- legal / compliance
- adoption / enablement

## Principle

Risk should remain live, reviewable, and historically visible throughout the delivery lifecycle.
