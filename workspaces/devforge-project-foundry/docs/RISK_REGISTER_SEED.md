# DevForge: Project Foundry
## Seed Risk Register

Status: Planning  
Date: 2026-03-28

| ID | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Owner | Mitigation Idea |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| R-001 | Template becomes too heavy and teams bypass it | Medium | High | TBD | Pilot with one or two teams and gather friction feedback before widening rollout |
| R-002 | Documentation drifts away from actual delivery behaviour | High | Medium | TBD | Design docs and schemas so more outputs can be generated or validated automatically |
| R-003 | Security or privacy review becomes a delivery bottleneck | Medium | Medium | TBD | Define baseline controls and clear review triggers early |
| R-004 | Comms layer becomes noisy or duplicative instead of useful | Medium | Medium | TBD | Define event-based comms rules and audience-specific outputs from the start |
| R-005 | Training generation is based on draft behaviour rather than approved truth | Medium | High | TBD | Restrict ForgeTraining to approved artifacts, validated review outcomes, and release-ready evidence |
| R-006 | Privacy concerns get absorbed into security and lose dedicated ownership | Medium | High | TBD | Keep Privacy Lead as an explicit role and log privacy-class risks separately in ForgeRisk |

## Notes

This is a planning seed only. Formal ForgeRisk structures and mitigation history are planned for MVP and later hardening phases.
