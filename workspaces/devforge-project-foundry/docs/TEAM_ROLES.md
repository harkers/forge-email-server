## Privacy Position

Privacy has its own dedicated role.

The Privacy Lead owns privacy-by-design expectations, DLP boundary concerns, privacy review input, and privacy-class risks within ForgeRisk. Privacy is not treated as an invisible sub-duty of security.

## Role Notes

- Human leads own decisions, sign-off, and accountability.
- AI-assisted specialists support analysis, drafting, packaging, and structured outputs.
- Final ownership remains human-led even where AI support is strong.
