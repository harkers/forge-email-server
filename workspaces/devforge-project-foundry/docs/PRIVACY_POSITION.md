# DevForge: Project Foundry
## Privacy Position

Status: Planning  
Date: 2026-03-28

## Privacy Has Its Own Role

Privacy is a dedicated function within Project Foundry.

It should not be treated as an invisible side-duty under security. Security and privacy work closely together, but privacy retains its own ownership, review voice, and risk visibility.

## Human and AI Support

- Human owner: Privacy Lead
- AI-assisted specialist: Privacy Analyst Agent

## Where Privacy Sits in the Lifecycle

Privacy is active in:
- Forge Architecture
- Forge Governance
- Forge Review
- ForgeTraining
- ForgeRisk

## Privacy Responsibilities

- privacy-by-design input during architecture
- data-handling expectations and minimisation logic
- DLP and sharing boundary concerns
- privacy review before closure or release progression
- privacy-class risk ownership in ForgeRisk
- privacy notes in training and communications where relevant

## Core Privacy Controls

- PRIV-01 Privacy and Data Protection
- DLP-01 DLP and Access Boundaries

## Principle

Privacy should be designed in early, reviewed explicitly, and visible in risk and governance records rather than discovered late as an afterthought.
